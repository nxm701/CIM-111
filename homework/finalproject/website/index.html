NIHAR MANDAPATY

ABOUT

Nihar Mandapaty is a Miami-based Music Producer originally from Chicago who composes and produces electronic music. As a musician he puts genuineness above all else, telling his own personal stories through beautifully crafted melodies and massive sweeping synth textures, sometimes juxtaposed with raw, powerful basslines. His distinct style is compared with the likes of Porter Robinson, Crywolf, and Owl City.

Mandapaty's goal as an aspiring industry leader is to develop himself as a musician while also making meaningful and lasting connections and gaining experience in as many fields as he can. His ultimate mission is to help drive positive change and innovation in the music industry.

Currently a sophomore studying Media Writing and Production at the Frost School of Music, Mandapaty has been producing music since 2014. Raised a classical musician, he has also played the trumpet for more than 9 years of his life and continues to play trumpet in the school of music.
