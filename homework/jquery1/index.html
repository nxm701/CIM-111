Step 1

Fold a piece of paper "hot dog" style.


Step 2

Fold the top corners downward to meet in the middle of the paper.


Step 3

Fold the top down to resstrongble an envelope. Leave a little bit of room at the bottom, about a half inch.


Step 4

Fold the top corners in again so they meet at the middle. There should be a small triangle beneath the folds.


Step 5

"Seal the envelope" by folding the small triangle up.


Step 6

Fold the entire paper in half outwards. The small triangle should be visible on the bottom.


Step 7

Finally, this is what it should look like! This type of paper airplane is relatively easy to make and can fly pretty far if done right.
